Brand Overview

You can discover our brand guidelines as well as downloadable assets here. You will also discover instructions on how to incorporate our assets into your own work.

On light backgrounds, the color logos look best.

In cases where more contrast is needed, you might opt to use the outlined version of the brandmark.

On dark and saturated backgrounds, the hollow white logos look best.

For monochromatic designs black and white versions are provided.

Take care to give the logo enough breathing room.

Take care to avoid stretching or adding effects to the logo.
